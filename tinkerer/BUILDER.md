## AI Journey & Philosophy

I’ve lived through the full evolution of AI — from asking questions in ChatGPT to orchestrating parallel autonomous agent teams. At CodeWalnut I’ve integrated AI into every engineer’s workflow, moving the team from prompt engineering to context engineering, shared rules/skills, and finally deterministic agent swarms with memory, retry mechanisms, telemetry, and human oversight.

I believe AI is infinite leverage — but only when guided by great engineers who deeply understand deterministic systems, architecture, and quality. AI is an incredibly fast junior developer that doesn’t care about maintainability, security, accessibility, or long-term craftsmanship. My role is to provide the vision, the standards, and the checkpoints that turn raw speed into reliable, production-grade software.

I’m a “show, don’t tell” leader. I experiment constantly (especially inside GrokClaw/OpenClaw), measure what actually moves the needle, then share practical workflows with the team through Loom videos and hands-on demos. The result is higher velocity, cleaner code, and engineers who are genuinely excited about what’s possible.

I’m energised by the massive opportunity ahead: bringing real AI automation to traditional businesses that are still doing repetitive work manually. The businesses that adopt agentic engineering fastest will win — and I want to be the one helping them do it safely, deterministically, and at scale.
